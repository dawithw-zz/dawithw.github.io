About me

I graduated with my Bachelors in Biology from the University of Florida. It was during this time I got introduced to the world of computer science. Having taken my first programming course, I was deeply intrigued and excited. I decided to learn more, so I added it as my minor.

Please don't lie!!!! Working in a business, however, was more rewarding that I expected. It gave me a great insight into how to handle working in teams, understanding different personalities and improving my accountability. During this time, I kept working on learning and developing apps. The more I spent time doing that, the clearer it became to me where I wanted my life to head. I found my passion.

I enrolled for my masters to get a little bit more formal education before diving into the industry. I learnt about design and architecture of enterprise systems, building interactive web applications, and writing clean testable code to streamline the software development process.

This is where my real journey begins.
